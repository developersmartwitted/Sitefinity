FEATURES

Multiple Project Management

Centrally plan multiple projects and access them from anywhere. With sem.PCP you can easily plan, manage, and organize an unlimited amount of projects. In addition to the planning and management capabilities, the operationalization of tasks is another important key area.

FUNKTIONSÜBERSICHT

Lean Management

Optimize your product development process. Increase the effectiveness of your planning, controlling, and management of your projects, while at the same time reducing costs in the individual areas. Sem.PCP supports you in digitally displaying your processes and in aligning your projects accordingly.

FUNKTIONSÜBERSICHT

Standardization of Processes

Plan and manage your projects using standardized methods and processes in order to achieve maximum effectiveness. Define your processes in sem.PCP following the proven mapping method and simply display your activities there. Complex dependencies of various sub processes/activities will be displayed visually in a simplified way in order to ensure the highest possible transparency at all times.

Plan & Manage & Collaborate

Increase your effectiveness through seamless integration of planning, management, and collaboration. sem.PCP optimally links these three areas and offers numerous functions for each individual area.

Plan your projects on the basis of standardized processes/phases and display the complexity in a project map. You are kept up to date through interactive dashboards and analyses and are therefore able to recognize and react to bottlenecks as soon as possible.
